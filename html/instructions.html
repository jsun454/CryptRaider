Crypt Raider Instructions

- You need to move the guy around the screen and push an orb into a portal to move to the next level.

- The main enemies are mummies and you need to aoid the enemies or else, you lose.

- There are bombs and if you touch the bombs, you need to get away from it as soon as possible or else you will get caught in the explosion.

- The only way to kill the mummies is for them to get caught in the explosion of a bomb.

- You can only move around in the yellow squares (soft sand)

- The are rocks and if you make contact with them, the guy moves the rock along the way, so you have to use logic and strategy to somehow get the orb into the portal.

Good Luck!
